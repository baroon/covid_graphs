Who are we?
We are one more in a community of people working on data modelling and statistical analyis of the pandemic from open and crowd sourced data. There is 'Nothing official about it', Aha!
What is the source of our data?
For the graphs, we are using data that is validated by a group of volunteers and published into a Google sheet and an API. API is available for all at api.covid19india.org. We thank the people at covid19india.org for making this data available. This data is augmented with other data and recalulated by us as per our requirements.
Why do we have data only for April onwards?
The data we rely on is a moving one i.e. it changes everyday even for previous dates. We however are interested in keeping a snapshot of the data for a particular day and comparing it with other snapshots to keep our trends static. We had only started accummulating data since April.
